About Udaan

UDAAN Program has been sketched by Dhyeya IAS to accommodate the young minds who have completed their class 12th and are headed towards graduation level.  This program has been in place for students aspiring to build a career as a Civil servant. The UDAAN program was launched by Dhyeya IAS with an aim to give wings to the dreams of young & strong minds, who are willing to put in the work and effort required to make their aspirations a reality. Team UDAAN understands the challenges associated with the preparation for competitive examination, the vast syllabus, the peer pressure etc. The UDAAN Program was started to tackle these problems at the very root of it.

The methodology followed by UDAAN Program provides guidance and mentorship to students paving way for them to clear exams like civil services and many more. Under the guidance of experienced and expert faculty UDAAN program provides comprehensive course curriculum equipping students to clear competitive exams like UPSC-CSE, PCS, EPFO etc.  This program facilitates such young aspirants who carry a lot of zeal & willpower, to start preparing at a young age & achieve an advantageous position to crack the exam on the very first attempt itself, after their graduation.
